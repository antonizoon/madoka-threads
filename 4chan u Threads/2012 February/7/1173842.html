>favorite crack pairing
Mami and Momo.

Set in a super-happy alternate universe where Homu has a brief moment of consciousness much earlier, saving Mami's family from the car crash by describing it thoroughly, and having Mami help Kyouko in return. Mami and Kyouko become friends after a heated philosophical debate where Mami ostensibly wants to buy salvation for money and Kyouko throws biblical quotes at her. Mami instead helps Kyouko into Mitakihara through a letter of recommendation, Kyouko is forced to study like she's never studied before, it lights her fighting spirit. The faint hope makes Kyouko's father get his act together.

Fast forward one year. Momo, Kyouko's little sister enters Mitakihara as a first grader, qualifying solely on her grades. She barely gets on the school grounds before being personally greeted by all the sports clubs, including Sayaka of the kendo team. From there's it's an exploration of Kyouko's various sports feats as the jack of all trades, and how she gained the nickname "Rosso Fantasma" in the last inter-school sports event. And why everyone expects Momo to have her sister's potential.

The tea ceremony club has Mami-neechan who always helps Kyouko with her homework. Can she tame the younger one?
